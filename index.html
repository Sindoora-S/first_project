REPRODUCTIVE HEALTH

SESSION 3: WORKSHEET

In vitro fertilisation(IVF), followed by embryo transfer (ET) is one of the techniques that include in Assisted Reproductive Health (ART).

In this method, ova from the female partner/donor(female) and sperm from the male partner/donor(male) are collected and are induced to form zygote under stimulated conditions in the laboratory.The zygote or early embryos with upto 8 blastomeres could then be transferred into the fallopian tube and embryos with more than 8 blastomeres, into the uterus to complete its further development.

Embryos formed by in-vivo fertilisation also could be used for such transfer to assist those females who cannot conceive.
